WHAT MOTIVATES ME?

As I mentioned earlier my ultimate goal is to take of those I care for but at times, just like any human being, I experience a burnout.
A burnout is characterized as “A state of complete mental, physical, and emotional exhaustion”.
Overcoming that feeling of burnout is extremely important as it may derail the goals I try to achieve.
Some methods I use to overcome that feeling include:

  • Hanging out with friends
  • Reading self-improvement books
  • Cleaning
  • Journaling
  • Watching this Motiviational video by Jordan Peterson
