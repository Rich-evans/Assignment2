WHO AM I?

I go by the name of Richard Evans and I am currently a student at Humber College enrolled in the Computer Programming and Analysis program. I grew up in a Caribbean household and have 1 sibling, that being my older brother, and we were both raised by my mother and grandmother. Growing up, I was never really a social child and that's why I took a liking to books. Reading has become one of my favorite past times as I find it so amazing that we can learn and read about a variety of topics and assimilate them into our own lives based on the perspective of another person.

As reading has become a hobbie of mine, so has basketball. My brother was a college basketball player when I was younger and just like any (annoying) younger sibling, I wanted to do whatever it was that he was doing. It soon became my own interest as I got older and I still continue to hold love for the sport until this day.




Another hobbie of mine is drawing. I used to love cartoons, specifically anime's, as a child and would always try to draw my favorite characters. Drawing was also a skill that my brother had so of course I wanted to do what he was doing lol. Along with those hobbies I enjoy watching sports and watching movies that are from the drama genre.
